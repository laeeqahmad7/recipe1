Ingredients

  • 1 (12-ounce) package orecchiette or small shells pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided.
  • 12 ounces fresh merguez or spicy Italian sauage, casings removed
  • 3 tablespoons spicy harissa (such as mina) , plus more to taste
  • 1/3 cup dry white wine
  • 1 print multicolored cherry tomatoes, halved (about 2 cups)
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salts, plus more to taste
  • 1 cup very thinly sliced zucchini and or yellow crookneck squash
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced fresh mint, plus more for garnish
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled (about 1 cup)
